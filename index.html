01 — The REALway Method

Training means educating.
Playing means growing.

REALway is Real Madrid Foundation’s unique football methodology — a model where sport becomes a path to personal development. The focus is on play and learning, not on talent.

Player

Technical and physical development through real game situations and structured training.

Game

Understanding football tactically and strategically — learning to read the game.

Mindset

Values, leadership, and personal growth — building character on and off the field.

02 — The Program

Four categories,
one shared mission.

Each age group follows an adapted curriculum designed for their developmental stage. Sessions take place at Centre Sportif de Woluwe-Saint-Pierre, Brussels.

U6–U8
Bambini
  • 2 sessions/week (60 min)
  • 5v5 format, small pitch
  • Focus: motor skills, fun, discovery
  • Tue & Thu 16:00–17:00
  • Saturday 10:00–11:00
U9–U11
Poussins
  • 2 sessions/week (75 min)
  • 8v8 format, medium pitch
  • Focus: technique, game understanding
  • Tue & Thu 17:00–18:15
  • Saturday 11:00–12:15
U12–U14
Minimes
  • 3 sessions/week (90 min)
  • 11v11 format, full pitch
  • Focus: tactics, physical dev.
  • Mon, Wed, Fri 17:00–18:30
  • Match day: Saturday PM
U15–U17
Cadets
  • 3 sessions/week (90 min)
  • 11v11 format, full pitch
  • Focus: competition, leadership
  • Mon, Wed, Fri 18:30–20:00
  • Match day: Saturday PM

04 — Events & Experiences

More than training.
Unforgettable experiences.

🏟️

World Challenge — Madrid

International tournament at Valdebebas. 72 teams, 23 nationalities. Our Belgium squad competes against EFP programs worldwide.

⚡

FRM Clinics

Special intensive sessions with Real Madrid Foundation coaches visiting Brussels. High-level training, exclusive content.

🏆

Internal Tournaments

Monthly competitive events between age groups. Trophy ceremonies, MVP awards, and team building activities.
